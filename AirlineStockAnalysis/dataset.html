Please use the above dropdown menu to select the Collective or Close Dataset. The Collective dataset includes Close Price, Volume, Open Price, High, and Low for 6 different airlines. The Close dataset includes only the close prices for the 6 airlines.

Airlines are identified by Stock listing in the datasets.

  • DAL - Delta Airlines
  • AAL - American Airlines
  • JBLU - Jetblue Airlines
  • LUV - Southwest Airlines
  • SAVE - Spirit Airlines
